Our Story

From a roadside stall to a family table.

Kamran Dhaba began as a single charpai under a peepal tree on the Grand Trunk Road, where Bibi Kamran cooked just two things — daal makhani and tandoori roti — for tired truck drivers heading to Lahore.

Three generations later, the recipes haven't changed. The spices are still ground fresh each morning. The biryani is still layered the night before. And every guest is still greeted the way Bibi greeted her first customer: "Aayiye, baith jaiye, garam khaana taiyyar hai."
